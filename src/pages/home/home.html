What is CryptoSafari?

CryptoSafari is a CryptoKitties inspired blockchaingame. You can buy,collect, breed and sell cute andadorable digital animals that are unique, secured onthe blockchain and owned by you.

Once you bought an animal, 90% of proceeds gotoward charities of your choosing. Curate your ownCharity Portfolio from over 50 world renownedcharities. The animal is now yours to keep, breed withto get new animals or you can sell it to someone else. Profits of your sales and breeding go to you.
